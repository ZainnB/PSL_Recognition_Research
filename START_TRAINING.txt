⚡ WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU RUN "python train.py"
────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

   - Training: 1,164 (70%)
   - Validation: 249 (15%)
   - Testing: 249 (15%)

2. Creates model with 4.8M parameters on GPU

3. Trains for 30 epochs with:
   - Batch size: 16
   - Learning rate: 3e-4
   - AdamW optimizer
   - CrossEntropy loss
   - Gradient clipping: 1.0

4. Saves:
   - Best model to checkpoints/model_best.pt
   - Periodic checkpoints every 5 epochs
   - Training metrics to logs/history_*.json

5. Displays per-epoch metrics:
   - Training loss & accuracy
   - Validation loss & accuracy
   - Per-class test accuracy at the end
